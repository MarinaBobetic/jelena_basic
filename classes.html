Masquerade Theater classes

Masquerade Theater classes offer a space to explore acting, theatre and character development.



BUT THEY ARE MUCH MORE THAN JUST ACTING CLASSES.

Through games, physical theatre exercises and playful somatic movement, we explore our personal stories, connect with our bodies and emotions to build confidence and to develop a powerful stage presence that can support us in many different contexts - as actors, musicians or public speakers, teachers/facilitators, or simply to get to know ourselves a little better.
