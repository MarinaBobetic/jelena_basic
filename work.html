WORKING WITH JELENA

Jelena is a dream to work with. Her brilliance, creativity, humility, and wisdom makes it easy to forget that she is still at the beginning of her career.

Peter Lorenz, director

Download CV

Recent body of work

Jelena was recently performing at the Edinburgh Fringe at ZOO Southside in a solo show Bambiland, directed by Peter Lorenz, for which she was nominated for the Filipa Bragança Award for Best Female Solo Performance by an Emerging Artist, and will continue to tour with this show.

She was also directing a show The Dream of Al-Andalus with Scottish Storytelling Centre.
